## Ondara – A Seminar in Tokyo (Microshow performance for The Current)
 - [https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nbbi-2r-Gwc](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nbbi-2r-Gwc)

On Tuesday, Oct. 25, 2022, Kenya-born, Minnesota-based singer-songwriter Ondara performed a Microshow at the Hook & Ladder Theater and Lounge in Minneapolis before an audience of attendees who had won passes to attend the event. The set included this performance of "A Seminar in Tokyo," off Ondara's latest album, "Spanish Villager No. 3."

## Joe Rainey - studio session and interview (live for The Current)
 - [https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=di0qFhdxnao](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=di0qFhdxnao)
 - RSS feed: https://www.youtube.com/feeds/videos.xml?channel_id=UCAKcSON6PSG_JkTbXh2WdIg
 - date published: 2022-12-05 00:00:00+00:00

Powwow singer Joe Rainey performed three tracks from the critically acclaimed 2022 record Niineta at The Current. With support from producer Andrew Broder, the two mix orchestral and experimental electronic soundscapes with gorgeous native chanting. Local Show host Diane talked with Rainey, a Minneapolis native now based in Wisconsin, about the inspiration for this unique collaboration, as well as his aspirations to uplift and amplify Native culture.
