## The Heavy Heavy – three-song performance at The Current
 - [https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=uSV1tkj_TXk](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=uSV1tkj_TXk)

While on tour across the United States, U.K. band The Heavy Heavy were living a real-life version of their hit song ‘Miles and Miles’ when they visited The Current studio for a session hosted by Ayisha Jaffer.

Blending elements of ‘60s folk, cosmic Americana and good-old rock and roll, The Heavy Heavy performed a lively set in the studio. Watch and listen to their three-song performance above.

Don't miss the band's complete session and interview with host Ayisha Jaffer, posted here: https://www.thecurrent.org/feature/2022/11/30/watch-the-heavy-heavy-performing-in-the-current-studio
