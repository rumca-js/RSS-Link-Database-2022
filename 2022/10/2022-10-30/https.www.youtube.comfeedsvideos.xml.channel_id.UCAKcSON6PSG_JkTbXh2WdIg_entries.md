## Neal Francis discusses "In Plain Sight" (interview at The Current)
 - [https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4oalW4Q4m8Q](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4oalW4Q4m8Q)
 - RSS feed: https://www.youtube.com/feeds/videos.xml?channel_id=UCAKcSON6PSG_JkTbXh2WdIg

Chicago musician Neal Francis visited The Current studio while on tour in support of his full-length "In Plain Sight" and its follow-up EP, "Sentimental Garbage." Following on the music, Neal Francis sat down with host Bill DeVille for a conversation about how the album came together during the pandemic, and how Francis and his bandmates rehearsed and recorded a lot of the songs in a church where Francis had been living.
