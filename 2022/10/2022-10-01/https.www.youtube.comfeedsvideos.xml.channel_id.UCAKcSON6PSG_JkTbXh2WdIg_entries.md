On the release of their new album, "Spark," Chicago band Whitney’s Julien Ehrlich and Max Kakacek visited The Current to play songs and to chat with host Mac Wilson. Dipping into their back catalogue, Whitney also performed the song "Valleys (My Love)" from their 2019 album, "Forever Turned Around."
