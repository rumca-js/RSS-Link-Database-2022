Some pretty fun news for you all! GRAMMY® voting starts today, and “Gardenview” is on the ballot!!! It’s in all the American Roots categories including Best Folk Album and Best American Roots Performance. 

THANK YOU to all the voting members for your consideration.

Here are all the categories you’ll find us in:
   Best Folk Album: "Gardenview" by Nataly Dawn
   Best American Roots Performance: "Have You Heard"
   Best American Roots Song: "Joy"
   Best Music Video: "Joy"
   Best Engineered Album, Non-Classical: Caleb Parker, Will Borza
   Producer of the Year, Non-Classical: John Schroeder, Ross Garren
   Best Immersive Audio Album: “Gardenview” (Immersive Edition)

Much love,
Nataly
