After releasing "A Legacy of Rentals" — his fifth solo album — in May of this year, Craig Finn is now on tour in support of the record. His first show took place at the Turf Club in St. Paul on June 28, and on that day, Finn and his band The Uptown Controllers visited The Current studio for a session hosted by Ayisha Jaffer. Watch the songs Craig Finn and the Uptown Controllers performed in that session followed by Finn's thoughtful interview with The Current's Ayisha Jaffer.
