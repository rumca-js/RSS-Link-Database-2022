## Luke LeBlanc – Down Low (live for The Current)
 - [https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=lqPHQWhCwfU](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=lqPHQWhCwfU)
 - RSS feed: https://www.youtube.com/feeds/videos.xml?channel_id=UCAKcSON6PSG_JkTbXh2WdIg

Minnesota-based singer-songwriter Luke LeBlanc recently released his fourth full album. Entitled “Fugue State,” the album was produced by musician and producer Erik Koskinen at Koskinen’s home studio in Cleveland, Minnesota.

Luke LeBlanc and his band recently visited the Radio Heartland studio for a session hosted by Mike Pengra. Watch them perform the song, "Down Low."

Find the full session here: https://youtu.be/5LRgajNz3cc
