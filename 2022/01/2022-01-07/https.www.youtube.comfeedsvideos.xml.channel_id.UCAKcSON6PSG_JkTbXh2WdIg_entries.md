In September 2020, Ron Gallo gathered with his bandmates in a studio in Nashville to record performances of four songs exclusively for The Current. Three of the songs are from Gallo's 2021 EP "Peacemeal," and the fourth track — which also appears on "Peacemeal" — is a 2020 rework of a song from Gallo's 2017 album, "Heavy Meta."

SONGS PERFORMED
0:00 " Wunday (Crazy After Dark)"
2:36 "Easter Island"
4:56 "Hide (Myself Behind You)"
8:01 " All The Punks Are Domesticated" (2020 Version)

PERSONNEL
Ron Gallo – guitar, vocals
Chiara D'Anzieri – bass, backing vocals
Dominic Billett – drums
