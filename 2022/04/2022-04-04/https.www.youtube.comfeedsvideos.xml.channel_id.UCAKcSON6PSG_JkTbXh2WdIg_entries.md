## Interview: Superchunk's Mac McCaughan on 'Wild Loneliness,' and pre-internet touring
 - [https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=b7FTXF8Flz0](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=b7FTXF8Flz0)

Following the release of their 2022 album 'Wild Loneliness,' Mac McCaughan of North Carolinian icons Superchunk joined Mary Lucia to talk about the band's early experiences playing in Minnesota, touring in pre-internet times, and his decision to lean into a more acoustic sound on this release.
