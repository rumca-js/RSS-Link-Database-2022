The Playdate is a tiny little handheld filled with wonder and games you can't find anywhere else. Understated 1-bit black and white visuals and quirky crank-turning gameplay give the Playdate a unique charm. But this isn't just a novelty device; the Playdate feels like it has lasting power thanks to its stellar design and open and inviting approach to game development that will undoubtedly lead to many more cool and interesting games after the first season wraps.

## 9 Easy Tricks To Defeat BOTW Minibosses
 - [https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=bOuy95qXIXY](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=bOuy95qXIXY)
 - RSS feed: https://www.youtube.com/feeds/videos.xml?channel_id=UCbu2SsF-Or3Rsn3NxqODImw

The past 5 years have given players time to come up with complicated glitches to take out Breath of the Wild foes in style, but instead we’re dialing it back a bit, and going with the easiest, simplest tricks to take on minibosses.

Tricks such as lassoing Lynels with your camera, making your weapons have permanent hitboxes, and hypnotizing guardians to stay still can help players out to overcome Breath of the Wild’s tougher foes.
