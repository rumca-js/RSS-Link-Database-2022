2022's Prey is a new take on the Predator franchise, this time on streaming. It is a great example of what streaming movies should be: instead of putting 200 million dollar IP on streaming instead of theaters, make smaller movies with that IP you would never make for theaters and put that on streaming. One potential issue with the movie is that it could be too simple, to a point where it becomes almost too predictable. So in today's Anatomy of a Failure, let's see how to avoid making a movie that is too simple.
