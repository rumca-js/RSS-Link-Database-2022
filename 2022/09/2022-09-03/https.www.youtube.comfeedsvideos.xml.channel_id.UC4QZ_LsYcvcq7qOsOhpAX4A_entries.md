When it comes to social media, our innocence has been lost. What was once fun and lighthearted has been riddled with scandals ranging from data breaches to depression and social division. What if there was a better way? A new player called BeReal has risen to be currently the most downloaded app in US. It’s the “anti-social media” social media. But will it last? Let’s take a look.
